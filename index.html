History of Google

  • 1996-2000:Google Growth

Their idea was officially founded on September 4, 1998. At first their project was named "Backrub" but later changed it to Google as a play on the mathematical symbol, the "googol." Page and Brin soon received their first investment for Google. Andy Bechtolsheim wrote a check for $100,000 to the two.
